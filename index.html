Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

ScanPro ("we", "our", or "the App") is a document scanning application for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This is used solely for authentication.
  • Scanned Documents: Documents you scan, import, or create are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, access, or store your documents on our servers.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screen views, feature usage, and app events. This data does not identify you personally and helps us improve the app.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic information to fix bugs and improve app stability.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy for details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To authenticate your identity when you sign in
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To display advertisements

3. Data Storage & Security

All scanned documents, images, and OCR text are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not transmit your documents to any external server. Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features including encryption and device passcode.

4. Camera & Photo Library

ScanPro requests access to your device's camera to scan documents and your photo library to import images. These permissions are used solely for the App's scanning and import features. Images captured or imported are stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config
  • Google AdMob: Advertising
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the data they collect.

6. Children's Privacy

ScanPro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your scanned documents from the App's Settings
  • Sign out and remove your account data
  • Revoke camera and photo library permissions via iOS Settings
  • Reset your advertising identifier via iOS Settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
